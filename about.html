About Us

About Simply The Best Barbers

Simply The Best Barbers is more than just a barbershop — it's a staple of community and style in Dun Laoghaire. With years of experience and a reputation built on quality and care, our team is passionate about helping every client walk out feeling fresh, confident, and like the best version of themselves. Whether it's a sharp fade, classic cut, or beard trim, our crew puts their all into every detail.

Meet the Crew

At Simply The Best Barbers, it’s not just about haircuts — it’s about people. On the left, Youcef brings precision and flair to every style he touches. Known for his sharp fades and laid-back vibe, he’s the guy clients trust when they want to look and feel fresh. On the right is Abdul, the owner and heart of the shop. His passion for the craft and commitment to great service is what built this place from the ground up. Being a family-owned business, there’s a sense of pride and community in everything they do — and you feel it the second you walk through the door.
